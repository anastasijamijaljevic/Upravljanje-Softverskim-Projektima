ABOUT OUR ASSOCIATION

The association "Čistije sutra" is the initiative of the students from the State University of Novi Pazar in order to increase civic awareness about the recycling opportunities in Novi Pazar

We are here to help people recycle more products as often as possible. We also teach them how to take full advantage of the product before they recycle it. We also want to raise awareness among people about the importance of environmental conservation and how they can utilize wastewater.

In addition to investing our income in educating our fellow citizens about recycling, we donate part of our income to local organizations that have a similar goal.

Our organization is a group of young students dedicated to promoting recycling and encouraging sustainable behavior in our community. Our goal is to raise awareness about the importance of recycling and provide practical guidelines for its implementation. Through our activities, we aim to educate and inform people about the benefits of recycling for the environment. We organize lectures, workshops, and campaigns at universities, schools, and public spaces to share information about proper waste sorting, identifying recyclable materials, and recycling procedures. Additionally, we actively collaborate with local businesses and authorities to improve recycling infrastructure in our city. We organize clean-up actions in public areas and collect recyclable waste to directly contribute to the preservation of our environment.
